P R O B L E M
Retail options: toys, scams, and a missing middle.
Across the US and India, the same gap: tools are either too simple to give edge, or too hyped to be honest.
The Loss Rate
India: 91% of retail F&O traders lose (SEBI 2024). US: ~70% of retail 
options traders lose (SEC). Same broken pattern, two geographies.
Toys for Retail
Robinhood, Streak: no vol surface, no Greeks aggregation, no 
honest performance reporting. Retail gets the broker, not the brain.
Scams Above
"AI signal" Discords, options-tip Telegram groups, alpha-promising 
YouTubers. The trust deficit is real and growing.
Missing Middle
Bloomberg-grade analytics sit behind $30K/year terminals. No 
honest, research-grade options platform exists at retail price points 
across both geographies.
Sources: SEBI Circular Feb 2025 · SEBI F&O Study 2024 · NSE Market Pulse Dec 2025 · OCC Retail Options Data
