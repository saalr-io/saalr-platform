# SAALR Seed One-Pager (Investor Version)

## What SAALR Is
SAALR is a research-grade options analytics platform for retail traders, built on an explicit trust-first promise: honest models, transparent reporting, and no "AI says buy" signals.

US-first, India-next.

## The Problem
Retail options traders face a broken market structure:
- Basic broker UX lacks institutional analytics depth.
- Hype-driven signal products overpromise and underreport failures.
- The retail segment lacks a credible middle layer combining rigor, usability, and affordability.

## The Product
SAALR combines:
- Vol surface + portfolio-level Greeks
- Sentiment-adjusted Monte Carlo probability of profit
- GARCH volatility forecasting
- FinBERT sentiment scoring
- LSTM shown against ARIMA baseline (no hidden model switching)
- Education funnel via OptionsAcademy (50+ modules)
- Multi-broker execution rails (US + India roadmap)

## Why It Can Win
Defensibility comes from integration depth, not model novelty:
- Honest model reporting (baseline vs model in product)
- Analytics + education + execution in one experience
- Multi-broker, multi-geography architecture
- Brand positioning in a market with growing trust deficit
