# SAALR One-Pager (Institutional Seed)

## Thesis
SAALR is building a trust-first, research-grade options analytics platform for retail traders in two large markets (US first, India next), with a validation-gated commercialization model designed to reduce false-positive growth and model-risk narratives.

## Problem
Retail options participation is high, but tool quality and trust are low:
- Broker-native tools are often shallow for serious options workflows.
- Signal products frequently rely on opaque claims.
- Institutional-grade analytics remains inaccessible at retail price points.

## Product and Architecture Advantage
- Unified analytics stack: vol surface, Greeks, POP, sentiment composition
- Explicit baseline governance: model vs baseline visible in product
- Multi-broker abstraction with compliance guardrails
- Retail-first architecture with cross-geo expansion path
- Evidence-linked claim governance embedded in platform design

## Execution Status
- Pre-revenue by design at this stage
- Three shipped codebases
- Core engine components built
- Validation phase in progress before broad paid scaling

## De-Risking Plan (Validation -> Monetization)
- Q3 2026: out-of-sample validation publish
- Q4 2026: first paid US cohort, measure CAC/retention/ARPU
- Q1-Q2 2027: India rollout with compliance sequencing
- Q3-Q4 2027: retention-backed scaling and Series A readiness

## Metrics Framework
Planning targets (not current actuals):
- M18: 5,000 free, 200 paid, ~$80K ARR
- M24: ~$1M ARR run-rate
- M30: ~$3M ARR target

Unit-economics targets are assumptions pending cohort data (ARPU/CAC/LTV to be validated in Phase 1 cohort).

## Capital Ask
- $2M seed
- 18-month runway
- Allocation: engineering/hiring 40%, growth 25%, sales/BD 15%, infra 10%, ops 10%

## Investment Fit
Best suited for funds that value:
- disciplined claim governance in AI-adjacent products
- cross-geo fintech infrastructure with compliance-aware sequencing
- execution-first teams with measurable milestone gates

## Hard Guardrails
- Non-custodial model
- No unvalidated signal claims
- No live trading before paper-validation gates
- Strict tenant and production-data access controls
