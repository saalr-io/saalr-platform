My recommendation: A (Heritage) is the truest match to Goldman / Jane Street — a serif wordmark is the most timeless, institutional choice and ages the best. A common, strong outcome is a hybrid: the Heritage serif wordmark + the Terminal payoff mark as the icon/favicon — wordmark for the brand, mark for the app.
